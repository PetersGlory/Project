THE FIRM

The firm was established in 1972 by Bada Zacchaeus Olafenwa as a Sole Practitioner firm but grew to a partnership in 1973 when Chief Festus Folarin Sonaike joined.

The firm grew steadily over the years with additional top executives to assist in policy formulation and execution. The firm consciously adopted a policy of engaging experienced and tested professionals as consultants.

In the forty-three year existence of the firm, we have carried out our professional practice without blemish, producing many Chartered Accountants and participating in the economic growth of our country, Nigeria.
